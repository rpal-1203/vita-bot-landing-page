About VitaBot

Born out of compassion and innovation, VitaBot is more than a machine — it's a life-saving companion. We are a group of dreamers and doers working to transform healthcare in India using the power of AI and robotics. Whether it’s real-time monitoring, emergency response, or elderly care, VitaBot is designed for Indian homes and lives.
