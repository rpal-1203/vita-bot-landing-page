About EMS

The AI-powered Employee Management System (EMS) is designed to streamline attendance tracking and monitor employee activity across various dimensions. It records real-time check-ins and check-outs using biometric, RFID, or facial recognition technologies, and calculates working hours on a daily, weekly, and monthly basis, including active system time and break durations. The system also tracks employee location within the office, identifying which floor they are on using Wi-Fi triangulation, RFID zone mapping, or CCTV-based movement detection. Advanced anomaly detection algorithms help identify ghost entries (where attendance is marked without physical presence), fake attendance (such as buddy punching), and multiple attendance records across different devices or locations. The model ingests data from access logs, system usage, and surveillance feeds, applying time series analysis and machine learning techniques to generate insights and flag suspicious patterns. It offers secure, role-based dashboards for HR and management, ensuring data privacy and compliance while providing actionable reports and alerts for efficient workforce oversight. lives.
